Our Story

We started small.
We stayed honest.

In the 1970, M Lakhamsi began as a trading business in Gujarat — procuring groundnuts from local farmers and finding buyers wherever the crop was needed. No shortcuts. No unnecessary middlemen. Just a product, a price, and a promise to deliver.

Over the following decades, that promise took us to 75 countries. We added sesame seeds, castor oil, spices, and pulses — always processing in-house, always building direct relationships. Along the way, we became a Star Export House, listed on the BSE and NSE, and built one of the few Indian agri-facilities with laser aflatoxin sorting technology.

Fifty years later, the business is larger. The supply chain is more complex. The promise is the same: you can verify everything we do.
