Our Story

In the 1970, a trading business began in Gujarat.
It never stopped growing.

M Lakhamsi began as a trading business in Gujarat — procuring groundnuts from local farmers and finding buyers wherever the crop was needed. No shortcuts. No unnecessary middlemen. Just a product, a price, and a promise to deliver.

Over the following decades, that promise took us to 75 countries. We added sesame seeds, castor oil, spices, and pulses — always processing in-house, always building direct relationships. Along the way, we became a Star Export House, listed on the BSE and NSE, and built one of the few Indian agri-facilities with laser aflatoxin sorting technology.

Fifty years later, the business is larger. The supply chain is more complex. The promise is the same: you can verify everything we do.

Five Decades of Growth

A legacy built one decade at a time.

1970

The Beginning — Gujarat

Founded as a groundnut trading business in Gujarat. Direct procurement from local farmers. First export shipments to neighbouring markets. The foundation of a direct-relationship model that continues today.

Expanding the Product Range

Added sesame seeds and castor oil to the portfolio. Built the first in-house processing unit. Began exporting to the Gulf region and Southeast Asia — markets that remain core to the business today.

1980s
1990s

Going Global — 50+ Countries

Expanded to European markets. Achieved Star Export House status. Added spices, pulses, and grains. Built relationships with institutional buyers in the EU and US who required documented quality standards.

BSE & NSE Listing

Listed on the Bombay Stock Exchange and National Stock Exchange — making M Lakhamsi one of the few Indian agri-exporters with publicly audited financials. Achieved ISO, Halal, and Kosher certifications in the same decade.

2000s
2010s

Technology & Compliance

Installed in-house laser aflatoxin sorting technology — one of the first Indian agri-exporters to do so. Achieved HACCP certification. Expanded to 75+ countries. Built NABL-accredited testing infrastructure.

What Drives Us

Three principles. Fifty years. No exceptions.

01

Proof Over Claim

We do not ask buyers to trust us. We give them the documentation to verify us. Lab reports, certificates, audited financials — everything is available without a phone call. If we cannot prove it, we do not say it.

02

Direct Relationships

From the farmer in Gujarat to the food manufacturer in Rotterdam — we maintain direct relationships at every point in the supply chain. No unnecessary intermediaries. No opacity. The same model we started with in the 1970.

03

Supply Continuity

Our pan-India procurement network and multiple port access means that when one region faces a difficult harvest, we source from another. Supply continuity is not accidental — it is built into how we operate. Fifty years of consistent delivery is the proof.
